      Management Team

      O3’s managing partners have worked as key players with large Wall Street financial institutions, bringing with them a wealth of experience in support of trading, risk management and operational processing solutions. The management team is composed of results-oriented “hands on” managers, motivators and team leaders. Each has earned the respect of their customers, superiors, peers and subordinates through leading by example, excellence of execution, commitment and strong team play in successfully delivering leading edge solutions.

      Kiran Beebireddy – President

      Kiran has over 20 Years of experience in the technology industry and has been instrumental in starting and successfully running technology services organizations. He is focused on the general management career track of the company’s leadership and development.

      He has successfully Implemented Infrastructure and cloud initiatives for major federal, financial and global Non-Profit institutions. Kiran is a pioneering Leader in the secular growth platforms of Data Analytics and Cloud Services. Vision and strategy is Kiran’s passion and he thrives on attracting and empowering great talent.

      Sunil Pallapolu – Chief Revenue Officer

      Sunil's breadth of technology and operations expertise and 20+ years of experience in healthcare, education and more recently in the Capital Markets and Financial Services sectors, drives our strategy, and our focus on innovation in delivering leading-edge solutions for our clients.

      He has built large scale trading and risk management systems, and has demonstrated expertise in building high performing hybrid onshore-offshore teams, servicing various capital markets businesses. He also has led multiple regulatory initiatives and applied innovation in the areas of Blockchain, Cryptocurrencies & Distributed Ledgers, Big Data, AI & Machine Learning and Business Process Automation.

      Sunil holds degrees in Medicine and Surgery from the AP University of Health Sciences in India, and a Masters degree in Computer Engineering, specializing in Machine Learning, from the University of South Carolina.

      Harvey Rand – Managing Director, Sales & Marketing

      Harvey Rand’s 25+ years of direct sales, management, marketing and leadership experience in the Capital Markets and Financial Services sector guides our client relationships. He has successfully initiated, grown and managed global sales organizations in a number of software product and financial consulting services firms including Summit Systems, Principia Partners, Fusion Systems Group, and Sybase.

      Harvey oversees new sales opportunities as well as O3’s growing client base. He is fundamental in aligning current and evolving client requirements with the professional services and strategic solutions the firm is providing. As an entrepreneur, Mr. Rand is charged with assuring O3’s direction closely maps with leading trading technologies direction, applications, and evolving markets. Mr. Rand has an undergraduate degree in marketing from the City of New York’s Bernard M Baruch College and an MBA in Finance from Columbia University.

      Tim Cockerill – Managing Director, Strategic Accounts

      Tim Cockerill has leveraged his leadership and management skills in developing large scale trading systems to create a highly disciplined approach for delivering practical solutions that help financial institutions meet their business and technology objectives.

      Mr. Cockerill worked with several leading financial institutions including Societe General, Merrill Lynch and Standard & Poors in delivering firm wide real-time, front-to-back office trading solutions for Derivatives and Treasury trading operations. In addition to providing guidance and advisory services to many of O3 ‘s clients, Mr. Cockerill has also participated in several high profile system and technology initiatives with high visibility O3 clients.

      Mr. Cockerill is a former Lieutenant in the US Naval Reserve and holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from SUNY Maritime College.
