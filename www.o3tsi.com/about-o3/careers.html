As one of the leading high profile business and technology consulting boutiques focusing on Capital Market Trading and Risk Management solutions, we constantly search for highly motivated people with the best skills, domain expertise, discipline and winning attitudes.       Our work environment supports and encourages an entrepreneurial outlook and independent thinking.
