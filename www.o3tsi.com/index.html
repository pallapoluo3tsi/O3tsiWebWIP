      Our Story

      O3 Technology Solutions began in 2004 as a boutique consulting services firm focused in the derivatives and risk management sectors. The firm specialized in delivering solutions that spanned front-office through back-office trading operations across a wide asset class. Our work often encompassed downstream processes and accounting, testing and technology migrations with both third party (Calypso / Misys / Murex / OpenLink and others) and proprietary platforms.

      An active integration practice tied this foundation to other financial systems in our client’s environments to address “end to end processes”. A large part of our work then as well as now encompassed testing.
